# Prepayment Marshalling Account Reference Panel

On the New Prepayment Marshalling form, use the Awaiting Marshalling panel to choose prepayment accounts that have outstanding balances and no amortization entries.

Use the filter box to narrow the panel by catalogue number, particulars, debit account, or transfer account. Selecting a row fills the Prepayment Account field on the form. The Prepayment Account field can still be used directly when the account is not selected from the panel.

When supporting business documents are available, their document titles appear below the prepayment account field. Select a title to preview the scanned file in the form, then close the preview when finished.
