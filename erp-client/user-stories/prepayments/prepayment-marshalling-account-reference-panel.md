# Prepayment Marshalling Account Reference Panel

## Story

As a finance user creating a prepayment marshalling record, I want to see prepayment accounts that have not yet produced amortization entries so that I can choose the correct account without leaving the form.

## Steps

1. Open the New Prepayment Marshalling form.
2. Review the Awaiting Marshalling panel on the left.
3. Filter the panel by catalogue number, particulars, debit account, or transfer account when needed.
4. Select an account from the panel.
5. Confirm that the Prepayment Account field is populated on the form.
6. Open any listed business document title to preview the scanned support file in the form.
7. Use the document details to complete the amortization periods and fiscal month fields.

## Expected Result

The selected account is applied to the marshalling form, the normal direct Prepayment Account search remains available, and related business documents can be previewed without navigating away.
