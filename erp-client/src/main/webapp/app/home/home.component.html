Current iteration of ERP system is designed to handle day to day data needs and scenarios for

  • Document management
  • Transaction Settlements
  • Management of prepayment accounts (Straight-line amortization only)
  • Management of Work in Progress Data
  • Management of fixed assets, (using straight-line and reducing balance depreciation methods)
  • Management of Lease accounts and Lease Reporting
