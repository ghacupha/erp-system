# Prepayment account details

The prepayment-account view now shows the key account fields in a compact grouped layout. Use it to confirm the recognition date, transaction link, dealer, debit and transfer accounts, and the prepayment amount.

Related business documents appear in the document panel on the right. These documents can come from the prepayment-account itself or from the linked settlement. Select a document title to preview the PDF directly on the page, which is useful when you need to compare the accounting record against the scanned source document before making changes.
